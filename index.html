About Me!

Actress, model, dancer and singer born in Tenerife, Canary Islands.

Once I finished high school I moved to Barcelona to study BA Hons Acting at the Institute of The Arts Barcelona a British performing arts school validated by Liverpool John Moores University where I have successfully completed my studies.

After graduating at the IAB I then moved to Madrid where I am currently based and represented by Ruth Franco Talent and working at a stage project called “STOP”, as well as some fashion editorials.
